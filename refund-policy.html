Refund Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Gridmark is a digital product with instant delivery. All purchases are generally considered final. However, we review refund requests on a case-by-case basis for verified issues.

1. How Gridmark works

When you purchase a Gridmark product, the following happens automatically and immediately:

  • New customers: An API key is generated and sent to your email, along with your initial credits.
  • Existing customers: Credits are added to your existing balance automatically.

Because delivery is instant and automated, we are unable to reverse the process once it has completed.

2. When refunds may be considered

We may issue a refund in the following situations:

  • Duplicate charges: If you were charged more than once for the same purchase due to a payment processing error.
  • Technical failure: If a verified technical issue on our end prevented you from receiving your API key or credits, and the issue cannot be resolved.
  • Service unavailability: If the API was non-functional for an extended period and you were unable to use your credits.

3. When refunds are not available

Refunds are generally not issued for:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Dissatisfaction with AI-generated output quality
  • Failure to use purchased credits
  • Loss of API key due to user negligence

4. How to request a refund

To request a refund, email hun389j@gmail.com with:

  • Your email address used at purchase
  • Order number (from your LemonSqueezy receipt)
  • Description of the issue

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 3 business days.

5. Refund method

Approved refunds are processed through LemonSqueezy and returned to the original payment method. Processing time depends on your payment provider and typically takes 5–10 business days.

6. Credit expiration

Purchased credits do not expire. Your credit balance remains available for as long as the Gridmark service is operational.

7. Contact

For refund inquiries or billing questions, contact hun389j@gmail.com.
